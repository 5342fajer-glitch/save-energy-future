Looking After Planet Earth

Let's use energy wisely today for a cleaner, greener tomorrow.

1. Introduction

Energy is what makes our lives easier. We use energy every day at home, in school, and in our community. But using too much energy harms our planet.

2. The Problem

We waste energy daily by leaving lights on, using AC unnecessarily, and charging devices constantly.

4. Solutions – What Can We Do?

  • Turn off lights when not in use
  • Use fans or AC only when needed
  • Unplug chargers after use
  • Use natural light during the day
  • Start energy-saving challenges in your class

5. Why It Matters

  • Saves money on bills
  • Reduces pollution and greenhouse gases
  • Conserves natural resources
  • Creates a healthier environment for all

6. Conclusion / Message

Small changes can make a big difference. Let's work together to save energy, protect our environment, and build a better future!
